Mouthwatering – Tropical fruits. Canary Islands

13 sep 2011

Documentary about the tropical fruits that are grown and consumed in the Canary Islands: papaya, mango, carambola, caimito, red mamey, bananas, and avocado. For each of these fruits, all the characteristics of cultivation, plant, fruit, medicinal properties, fruit presentation, and tasting recipes are explained.

In Spain, the Murcia orchards are traditionally known as a reference for fruit and vegetable cultivation. The Canary Islands are mainly known for their banana production. However, unknown to many, the Canary Islands probably produce the best crops of tropical and exotic fruits at the national level. This is mainly due to two reasons: 1st the sunny and tropical climate for most of the year. 2nd its maritime climate influenced by the Atlantic Ocean. These two conditions create fruits with great aroma and intensity.
