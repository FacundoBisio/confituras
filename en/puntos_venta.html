At Palmelita we have been making Canary jams since 1968, in our workshop in Bajamar (Tenerife). The brand was born with the drive of master pastry chef Werner Bittermann and, since then, we have maintained a simple idea: to make honest products, with real flavour and with the care of artisanal production.

Our specialty is tropical fruit jams, worked with selected tropical fruits. We seek that each jar preserves the identity of the fruit: its aroma, its texture and that point of balanced sweetness that invites you to repeat.

Papaya, mango and guava are part of our best-known flavours, along with other varieties. At Palmelita we believe in the simple: good raw materials, careful recipes and a way of working that respects tradition.

What defines us

• Canary origin: a brand born in Tenerife, with local identity and international projection.

• Artisanal production: we take care of the process to achieve stable, tasty and consistent jams.

• Tropical fruits: we value flavours such as papaya, mango and guava in recipes that highlight the fruit.

• Quality and detail: we seek a balanced end result for breakfasts, desserts and pairings.

How to enjoy Palmelita

• Breakfasts: toast, croissants, yoghurt or porridge.

• Cooking: glazes, soft sauces, fillings and pastries.

• Cheeses: combines very well with semi-cured or fresh cheeses.
