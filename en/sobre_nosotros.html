Palmelita was born in 1968, founded by master pastry chef Werner Bittermann in his small workshop in Bajamar, Tenerife.

Deep knowledge of traditional Central European recipes combined with the excellent fruits grown in the Canary Islands made this product stand out from the very beginning for its outstanding quality.

Since then, our product philosophy has not changed: only the best selections, no preservatives or artificial colorants, using tropical, Canarian, and seasonal fruits such as guava, mango, pineapple, passion fruit, prickly pear, red fig, and more. An excellent product — a product from the Canary Islands.

“so much more...
than a jam”

At Palmelita, we have been making Canary Island jams since 1968 in our workshop in Bajamar (Tenerife). The brand began with the drive of master pastry chef Werner Bittermann and, ever since, we’ve kept a simple idea: make honest products with real flavor and the care of artisan craftsmanship.

Our specialty is tropical fruit jams, prepared with carefully selected fruit. We want every jar to keep the fruit’s identity: its aroma, its texture, and a balanced sweetness that makes you come back for more.

Papaya, mango, and guava are among our best-known flavors, along with other varieties. At Palmelita we believe in simplicity: great ingredients, thoughtful recipes, and a way of working that respects tradition.

What defines us

• Canarian origin: a brand born in Tenerife with a local identity and international reach.

• Artisan production: we care for every step to achieve stable, flavorful, consistent jams.

• Tropical fruits: we highlight flavors like papaya, mango, and guava in recipes that let the fruit shine.

• Quality & detail: we aim for a balanced result for breakfasts, desserts, and pairings.

How to enjoy Palmelita

• Breakfast: toast, croissants, yogurt, or oatmeal.

• Cooking: glazes, gentle sauces, fillings, and baking.

• Cheese: pairs beautifully with semi-cured or fresh cheeses.
