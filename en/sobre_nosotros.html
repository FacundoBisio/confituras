Palmelita Jams was born in 1968 from the hands of master pastry chef Werner Bittermann in his small workshop in Bajamar, Tenerife.

His deep knowledge of traditional Central European recipes combined with the excellent fruits grown in the Canary Islands made this product stand out from the very beginning for its high quality.

Since then, the product philosophy hasn't changed: only the best selections, without preservatives or colorants, using tropical fruits from the Canary Islands and seasonal fruits such as guava, mango, pineapple, passion fruit, prickly pear, red fig, and more. An excellent product, a product of the Canary Islands.
